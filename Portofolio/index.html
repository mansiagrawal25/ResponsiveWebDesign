I am Mansi

CocktailDB is a cocktail search and recipe resource, with over 500 amazing recipes. We are adding more ingredient everyday and we plan to make it one of the biggest community of Cocktail enthusiasts. The Goal of CocktailDB is to allow Cocktail enthusiasts to explore and try out new drinks and have a good time. CocktailDB is founded by cocktail and wine enthusiast and software developer Mansi Agrawal. Mansi is a Northeastern Graduate student, who enjoys writing code and building new things while trying out amazing recipes for cocktails. Check her LinkedIn profile and shoot an email if you would like to get in touch.
